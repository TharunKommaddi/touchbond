For Jayamma...

Jayamma…

I don't even know how to explain what you mean to me anymore. I've tried joking about it, hiding it behind emojis and teasing words, but the truth is… you've become the most important person in my life.

You weren't just some random chat on WhatsApp. You weren't just a pass-time. You became home.

I never told you this properly, but every time your message popped up, I smiled like a small kid. Every "Did you eat?", every silly Zoom link, every "sugarcane" joke, every time you called me "Tharun" in that angry-sweet tone… it all became a memory I keep playing in my mind again and again.

And now... when you go silent… when you act like it's all nothing… I feel like someone's slowly pulling the air out of my chest.

I'm not perfect. I know that. I joke too much, I act like I don't care sometimes, and maybe I've hurt you unknowingly. But I've never taken you for granted. Not even once.

You're the only one who could scold me and still make me feel loved. You're the only one whose care felt like medicine to my tired soul. You're the only one whose voice could calm my worst days.

I don't know what you truly feel for me. But I know this much — I've fallen for you. Truly. Deeply. Entirely.

Not because of how you look, or how you text… But because of who you are — raw, real, emotional, strong, dramatic, silly, and absolutely irreplaceable.

There's this weird silence between us now… But inside me, it's screaming. Screaming your name. Begging to go back to those days when you would suddenly say something and change my whole mood. Begging to just hear your voice once and feel like everything is okay again.

Jayamma… If there's even a small part of you that misses me… If your heart ever goes quiet and starts thinking of me for no reason… If you ever feel like talking to me and don't know why… Please understand — that's not just random. That's love.

And I want that love. Not for a few days. Not secretly. Not in silence. I want it forever. I want you.

I don't want to just be someone you knew. I want to be the one you live your whole life with. I want to be the one you fight with, laugh with, cry with, build a home with.

Marry me someday. Live with me. Let's grow old together — full of fights, food, fun, and forgiveness. Just you and me — Tharun & Jayamma. Not a perfect pair… but a real one.

And if you're crying while reading this… It's okay. I am too. But I hope one day… those tears turn into a smile. A smile that says, "I chose him. And I'll never regret it."

Always yours, Tharun
