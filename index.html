For Jayamma...

My dearest Jayamma,

You used to tell me to find someone else — someone who could give me a peaceful life. You said I deserved better. But, Jayamma, don't you see? That better person is you. You've always been the one.

You didn't need to understand everything at once. You just needed to let your heart speak — and you did.

You feared love would bring pain. But I'm here to prove love can be your healing.

From today, and every day forward — I'm not going anywhere. You're not alone anymore.

With all my heart,
Your man. Your husband. Your forever.
