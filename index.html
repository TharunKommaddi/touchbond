For Jayamma...

Jayamma…

I don't know how to even start this. I've written, deleted, written again… but nothing feels enough. Because what we had… what I felt… was not just chatting. It was life.

From the first "hi" till today… you became my favorite person, without even trying. And I don't think you even realize how deeply you touched my heart. How your voice… your messages… your scoldings… your silly emojis… became a part of me.

Every moment with you was a story. Every silence between our messages was a conversation. Every late night call was a journey we took together.

Do you remember all those random late night Zooms? Where I was sleepy but still stayed… just to hear your laugh? Do you remember how I used to wait for your message? Even a single "hmm" from you made my whole day better.

That one day when you got emotional and said "nuvvu emanna cheste gattiga cheptha"… Jayamma, I swear, I smiled so hard and my heart felt so warm. Because in your anger, there was so much care.

You called me names, teased me, said "nuvvu busy ley", "baaduga matladaku", but deep down I always knew… you were feeling everything, just like I was.

The way you would suddenly switch from telugu to english, The way your voice would crack when you were truly emotional, The way you would laugh at my stupid jokes, All of it became my most treasured memories.

Jayamma… You're not just someone I liked. You're not just someone I miss. You are the person I dreamed of living with.

Not just in a house, but in a life. A life where you yell at me for not eating… Where you fight with me and don't talk for 2 days, then suddenly send "ok fine" like nothing happened. A life where we laugh at stupid things… where you say "naa mood sarigga ledhu" and I hold you tight.

I know we joked a lot. I acted silly… laughed too much… tried to hide my feelings. But the truth is — I fell in love with you. Fully. Honestly. Completely.

And now… When your messages are not coming… when you don't tease me… when I scroll up just to read our old chats… I feel like a piece of me is missing.

I still remember that sugarcane joke. That tomato chat. That "take rest ra babu" voice note. I play them again and again when I miss you. And trust me — I miss you every single day.

Jayamma… I don't want anything big. I don't want expensive gifts or fairy tale love. I just want you.

The real you — with your mood swings, your anger, your tears, your laughter. Because even when you were mad, you made me feel more loved than anyone ever did.

If you're reading this now… and your heart is a little heavy… If your eyes are wet and you're remembering our silly chats, please know — you meant everything to me. You still do.

You are not a "past" for me. You are my "maybe forever".

And I'm still here… Waiting. Hoping. Praying.

That one day… you'll message me and say, "Tharun, come. Let's live this life together."

Until then… I'll keep loving you in silence.

Forever yours,
your Tharun
